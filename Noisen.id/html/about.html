About Our Branding

Noisen has the meaning where 'noi' comes from the word 'noise,' which means sound, while 'sen' comes from the word 'sensor.' When combined, it is a sound sensor. Noisen is an electronic device designed to detect the level of noise or sound in a specific environment.

About Our Product

A sound sensor is used to measure and monitor the level of noise in an environment. This information can be utilized to assess the quality of the environment, identify potential noise issues, and design strategies for noise management.
